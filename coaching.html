Flexible Coaching Program

  • The Program Includes:

    • Personalized Training Plan: Your training plan is tailored to your specific goals and needs by a NASM-Certified personal trainer, ensuring you get the most effective workouts for your fitness level and objectives.
    • App-Based Program Management: All your workouts and progress tracking are managed through the MyPTHub app, providing a seamless and convenient experience.
    • Scheduled Workouts & Reminders: Stay on track with scheduled workouts and timely reminders to keep you motivated and consistent.
    • Workout Completion Monitoring: We monitor your progress to ensure you're staying on track and meeting your goals, offering support and adjustments as needed.
    • Regular Check-Ins & Communication: Consistent check-ins and open communication help keep you motivated and address any concerns or questions you might have.
    • Instructional Tools & Videos: Access a range of instructional tools and videos that provide additional support and guidance throughout your fitness journey.

  • Optional Add-Ons:

    • In-Person Training Sessions: Available outside or at your location in the Powell/Cody vicinity for less than personal training rates at the bigger gyms ($50/hour).
    • Monthly Nutrition Plan: Custom nutrition plans to complement your training and that complement your dietary preferences and restrictions ($49/month).
    • 30-Minute Zoom Coaching: Additional coaching sessions via Zoom to provide extra support ($25).

  • Benefits of the Flexible Program:

    • Flexibility: Train on your schedule with the convenience of online management and optional in-person sessions.
    • Personalization: Each plan is tailored to your unique goals, ensuring you get the most effective and relevant workouts.
    • Support: Regular check-ins and communication keep you motivated and on track, with additional support available as needed.
    • Comprehensive: Combining fitness training with optional nutrition plans and extra coaching for a well-rounded approach to your health.
