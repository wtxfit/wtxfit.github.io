Welcome to WTX Mountain Fitness

Outdoor Proficiency, Preparation, and Performance

What we do in a nutshell: Training for outdoor pursuits and unexpected challenges.
Conquer all the outdoor adventures Wyoming and Montana have to offer with confidence by breaking down the barriers to activities like backpacking, hunting, or skiing. While many enthusiasts seek to elevate their skills to new heights, others are just starting their journey. Our Outdoor Performance Training program is designed for seasoned adventurers looking to push their limits with advanced wilderness skills, survival training, and optimized fitness. At the same time, we offer foundational classes to help beginners develop the necessary skills and fitness levels to begin their outdoor adventures. If you don't know where to start, you're in the right place, and our courses will equip you with the knowledge and preparedness to venture into the wild with confidence and make every experience a rewarding journey.
Whether you're aiming for elite performance or just getting started, our tailored programs provide the knowledge, training, and support to master planned adventures and navigate unplanned situations. Join us to elevate your outdoor experience and make every adventure a rewarding journey.

Pricing and Details

Outdoor Performance Training

Transform your experience with Outdoor Performance Training, an elite-level program designed for seasoned adventurers and high achievers. Our comprehensive training and personalized coaching will help you conquer your most ambitious outdoor goals while building advanced survival skills and maintaining lifelong fitness.

  • Personal Training - Elevated

    Custom workout programs are periodized and combine functional training with the natural environment to maximize performance. With a focus on HIIT, strength conditioning, and endurance, workouts are specifically designed to complement activities like mountain climbing, trail running, and extreme sports. Coaching sessions are in-person or online based on your preference and location.

  • Advanced Nutrition Coaching

    Designed to fuel peak performance and long-term health, we provide personalized plans that focus on dialing in your "macros" and tailoring nutrition to your specific dietary needs and goals. We stay away from fads and focus on the fundamentals.

  • Wilderness & Survival Skills

    Weekly lessons that include desert survival, reading topo maps, plant identification, putting up a tent, and anything/everything in between. We'll give you all the resources you need to ensure you're prepared for all your outdoor adventures (planned or unplanned).

  • Monthly Adventure Challenges

    Test your skills and push your limits with our real-world monthly challenges, designed to enhance your outdoor proficiency and endurance. Each challenge offers a unique opportunity to apply your training in diverse settings, ensuring continual growth and excitement.

  • Monitoring & Support

    Stay connected and on track with our comprehensive monitoring and support system. Through the dedicated app, you'll receive personalized feedback, track your progress, and have 24/7 access to reach out with any questions or concerns. We're here to support your journey every step of the way.

Flexible Coaching Program

Online Core with Optional In-Person Support

Our online-based coaching program is designed for individuals who want a customized fitness plan primarily delivered online, with the flexibility to add in-person sessions if desired. Whether you're looking to get in shape, maintain your fitness, or take the first steps towards a healthier lifestyle, this program offers the flexibility and support you need. Workouts can be done your way- on your schedule and either at home, at the gym, or on the go.

  • What's included?

    Our program includes a personalized training plan consistent with your goals, managed through the dedicated app. You'll receive scheduled workouts and reminders, and we'll monitor your workout completion to keep you on track. Regular check-ins and communication ensure you stay motivated, while instructional tools and videos provide additional support.
