Welcome to WTX Mountain Fitness

A new way to think about fitness

Embark on all the outdoor adventures Wyoming and Montana have to offer with confidence by breaking down the barriers to activities like mountaineering and skiing. Many would-be-enthusiasts face challenges due to a lack of skills or fitness levels, hindering their enjoyment of these exhilarating pursuits. Our tailored programs are designed to empower individuals, providing the necessary skills and fitness training to unlock a world of outdoor possibilities and make every adventure a rewarding experience.
Training starts March 2024. Call or email now to schedule your free consultation, get booked, and get ready for summer.

WTX Weekend Adventures

Guided day camps around the Bighorn Basin for beginner and intermediate-level adventurers utilizing a holistic approach of learning outdoor skills and bushcraft with an awareness of the physical requirements to perform in rugged terrain or tough situations. The mission changes often, so click HERE to see the schedule and sign up.

Not-Just-Personal Training & Nutrition

Custom programs designed for your individual goals that take a more comprehensive approach, including incorporating outdoor skills and learning how to be ready for anything. Training sessions can be outdoors or at your location. Remote/online coaching is also available.

Courses

From concise 2 and 4-week courses focused on boosting your fitness for high-altitude exploration to extensive programs blending fitness, nutrition, wilderness skills, and at-home preparation, we have everything you need to fully equip yourself for the great outdoors. Embark on a journey of transformation and readiness, tailored to elevate every aspect of your adventure experience.

Hi! Nice to meet you.

I'm Kim and I'll be your guide. I created WTX Mountain Fitness after I noticed there is a substantial divide between people who take advantage of the amazing area in which we call home, and people who don't, mostly because they lack the confidence to venture into the wilderness. That lack of confidence is attributed to not having the physical conditioning and ability, or not having the knowledge to stay safe and navigate effectively. I've been wandering the hills solo my entire life and want to bring that confidence to others by doing what I love.
