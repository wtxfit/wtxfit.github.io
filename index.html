A Quick Introduction

A new way to think about fitness

Embark on all the outdoor adventures Wyoming and Montana have to offer with confidence by breaking down the barriers to activities like mountaineering and skiing. Many would-be-enthusiasts face challenges due to a lack of skills or fitness levels, hindering their enjoyment of these exhilarating pursuits. Our tailored programs are designed to empower individuals, providing the necessary skills and fitness training to unlock a world of outdoor possibilities and make every adventure a rewarding experience.

WTX WEEKEND ADVENTURES

Guided day camps around the Bighorn Basin for beginner-intermediate level adventurers utilizing a holistic approach of learning outdoor skills and bushcraft with an awareness of the physical requirements to perform in rugged terrain or tough situations. It's also super fun and we get to make s'mores.

ALPINE ACTIVITY TRAINING

Personal training kicked up a notch. In order to thoroughly enjoy all the mountains have to offer (and to do it safely), you need strength, endurance, and stability. Curated workouts based on your goals ensure you'll be fully prepared for the challenges of high-altitude adventures.

PERSONAL TRAINING & NUTRITION

Custom programs designed for your individual goals that take a more holisticem> approach. Training sessions can be outdoors or your location. Remote/online coaching also available if you'd prefer to do your own thing.
